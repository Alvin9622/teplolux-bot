question: How does delivery work?
category: orders
keywords: delivery, shipping, dostavka, yetkazib berish, доставка, отправка
---

We deliver across the country to your address or the nearest pickup point.
Within the city delivery usually takes 1–2 business days; to the regions
3–7 business days. Delivery cost depends on the destination and order weight and
is confirmed by an operator when the order is placed. Pickup from our showroom
is always free.
