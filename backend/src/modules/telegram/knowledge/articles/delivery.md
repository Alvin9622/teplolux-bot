# Delivery

We deliver across the country to your address or the nearest pickup point.

- **Within the city:** typically 1–2 business days.
- **Regions:** typically 3–7 business days, depending on the destination.
- **Large equipment** (boilers, tanks) is delivered by a dedicated courier and
  can include unloading to the entrance.

Delivery cost depends on the destination and order weight and is confirmed by an
operator when the order is placed. Pickup from our showroom is always free.
