# About Teplolux

Teplolux is a leading supplier of heating and water-supply systems. We deliver
boilers, radiators, floor-heating systems, water heaters and pumps from trusted
European brands, and provide professional installation and after-sales service.

- 20+ brands
- 1000+ products
- 200+ partners across the region

Our mission is to make reliable heating accessible, with quality products and
honest, expert support at every step.
