# Payment

We support convenient and transparent payment options:

- **Cash** on pickup or delivery.
- **Bank card** (in the showroom or via a secure payment link).
- **Bank transfer** for companies, with an official invoice and contract.
- **Installments** are available for selected products via partner banks.

Prices are confirmed by an operator before the order is finalized. For a price
request, use the **Request Price** button on the relevant product page.
