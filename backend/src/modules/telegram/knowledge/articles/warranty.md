# Warranty

All products come with an official manufacturer warranty (typically 2–5 years,
depending on the model and brand).

- Keep your proof of purchase to use the warranty.
- Warranty covers manufacturing defects under normal use.
- Professional installation by our technicians keeps the warranty valid.

For warranty questions or claims, contact an operator or leave a service request
and we will guide you through the process.
